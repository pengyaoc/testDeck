Why is BMI measurement useful?

This measurement combines the inforamtion of height and weight to give a much better estimate on your weight condition. If you are taller than most people, it makes sense for you to be heavier as well. Not so much when you are shorter than average.

A man with 5 feet 9 and 132 pound has a bmi of 

703 * 132 / (5 * 12 + 9) ^ 2

BMI Categories

  1. Underweight less than 18.5

  2. Normal weight equals 18.5–24.9

  3. Overweight equals 25–29.9

  4. Obesity greater than 30
